## üë• Case Management

### Managing Your Caseload

#### Caseload Overview
- Access comprehensive client information across all systems
- View priority alerts and overdue tasks
- Monitor upcoming appointments and deadlines
- Track client progress and outcomes

#### Adding New Clients
1. Navigate to Case Management dashboard
2. Click "Add New Client" button
3. Complete client intake form:
   - **Basic Information**: Name, contact details, date of birth
   - **Case Details**: Risk level, case manager assignment
   - **Special Needs**: Disability status, medical conditions
   - **Emergency Contact**: Contact person and phone number
4. Click "Create Client" to add to system

#### Client Profile Management
Each client profile includes:
- **Personal Information**: Contact details, demographics
- **Case History**: Timeline of interactions and services
- **Housing Status**: Current housing and applications
- **Employment Activities**: Job applications and employment history
- **Legal Matters**: Active cases and court dates
- **Benefits Status**: Enrollment status and applications
- **Service Connections**: Referrals and service provider relationships
- **AI Recommendations**: System-generated suggestions for client support

### Task and Appointment Management
- **Smart Reminders**: AI-powered task prioritization
- **Calendar Integration**: Schedule and track appointments
- **Follow-up Tracking**: Monitor required actions and deadlines
- **Progress Documentation**: Record client interactions and outcomes

- **Emergency Shelters**: Immediate housing assistance
- **Supportive Housing**: Long-term housing with services

## ü§ñ AI Assistant Features

### Real-Time Resource Discovery

#### Using the AI Chat
1. Navigate to AI Chat (`/ai-chat`)
2. Ask natural language questions:
   - "What housing options are available in Los Angeles?"
   - "What are my tasks for today?"
   - "Find employment services for my client"
   - "What benefits is John eligible for?"

#### AI Capabilities
- **Real-Time Search**: Live search using Google and SERPER APIs
- **Context Awareness**: Understands client-specific needs
- **Multi-Resource Discovery**: Housing, employment, legal, benefits, social services
- **Verified Information**: Uses real search results, never fabricates data

#### Example Queries
```
User: "Find sober living houses in Miami"
AI: Searches real-time resources and provides:
- Facility names with contact information
- Addresses and phone numbers
- Program details and requirements
- Background-friendly policies
```

### Empty Context Handling
The AI system gracefully handles situations with no available data:
- **No Tasks**: "You currently have no tasks scheduled. Would you like me to help you create some tasks or check your client caseload?"
- **No Clients**: Offers to help with client intake
- **No Data Available**: Acknowledges honestly and offers assistance

## üè• Benefits Coordination
